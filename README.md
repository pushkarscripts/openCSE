openCSE is a clean and structured collection of Computer Science and Engineering notes. It provides semester roadmaps, subject explanations and revision-friendly content created specifically for college students.

openCSE aims to simplify college-level CSE learning. Instead of scattered PDFs and inconsistent material, it offers organized notes and topic breakdowns aligned with common university syllabi.

## üéØ Why openCSE?

Many students face unclear notes, messy resources or overly technical explanations. openCSE focuses on:

Ideal for first-year and second-year CSE students, or anyone learning CS fundamentals.
